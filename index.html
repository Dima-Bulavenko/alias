Alias
The task of each player is to explain as many words as possible to their teammates in a limited time.
During the explanation, it is forbidden to use the root of the word, to make sounds similar to the word, and to translate from foreign languages.
A correctly guessed word brings the team one point, and for a missed word the team gets a penalty (depending on the settings).
The winner is the team that reaches a predetermined number of points.
